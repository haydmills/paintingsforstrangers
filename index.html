PAINTINGS FOR STRANGERS

Who are we?

We are an organization that gives away original paintings to strangers, completely for free. We work with and support a selection of hand-picked artists to make our mission possible.



How does it work?

We distribute donations evenly to our artists in the form of art supplies. Our artists are committed to using these supplies to create paintings that will be given away for free to complete strangers. Although we will give some paintings away sporadically, we will have a "mass-giveaway" event every six months. Our operations are currently limited to Salt Lake City, Utah, but we will also occasionally give you a chance to get a free painting via our website and Instagram, so be on the lookout!



Sometimes we will also raise funds by selling our artists' original paintings on our website, so you will also have the chance to buy original art from our website while supporting our cause.



Sign up to receive email updates on painting giveaways and fundraising events!





Our Purpose

We want to further the understanding, awareness, and appreciation of art, free expression, and philosophy in our modern society. We want to expose people to an underground art movement that most would have never experienced. We want to brighten someone's day. We want to open minds in a society that is increasingly letting itself get distracted from the beauty of life and the universe.

Our Artists

Alex Lucas from Salt Lake City, Utah, he is known as the pioneer of neo-surrealist-psychedelic-cubism. He is currently the only known artist who creates art of this type, and decided to create the art movement after being dissatisfied with all others. His preferred mediums are acrylic paints, oil paints, and spray paint.





"Murder" an established Graffiti artist with almost ten years of experience, he has moved towards a more progressive style influenced by impressionism and surrealism. He is frequently engaged with painting commissioned murals on entire walls. When he is not busy making murals you will quite often find him doing artistic studies of the human body, women in particular. Due to his art form, he must remain under his pseudonym on this website. His preferred mediums are spray paint and acrylic paint.





Sir Omzi is a mysterious Graffiti artist who only paints in the abstract, his style is undefinable and is based on the absurdist philosophy. He makes more canvases than anything else, but also does wall murals. Due to his art form, he must remain under his pseudonym on this website. Although he uses acrylic paints the most, he says his preferred medium is plant matter.
